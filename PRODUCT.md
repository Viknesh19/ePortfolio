# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and technical interviewers reviewing Vikneshwaran's readiness for IT, software, data, and support roles. They are scanning quickly for credibility, skill fit, project proof, communication quality, and signs of practical delivery.

## Product Purpose
This portfolio presents Vikneshwaran as a modern Information Technology graduate with hands-on project experience across web systems, mobile apps, data analytics, automation, and IT operations. Success means a recruiter can understand the value proposition within the first viewport and confidently continue into projects, education, and contact.

## Brand Personality
Technical, precise, energetic. The site should feel like a polished IT command center, not a generic student template.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic Bootstrap portfolio layouts, flat icon-card grids, low-contrast gray text, cramped project cards, hover-only interactions, and decorative motion that slows down scanning.

## Design Principles
- Lead with proof: surface role, skills, project outcomes, and credibility before decorative content.
- Make scanning effortless: each page should have a clear intro, strong hierarchy, and short evidence blocks.
- Keep the tech vibe purposeful: use system panels, data-like structure, and crisp motion without hiding content.
- Respect every screen size: no clipped cards, horizontal overflow, or hover-only access on mobile.
- Recruiter trust beats spectacle: animations and visuals should support readability and confidence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, meaningful image alt text, reduced-motion support, keyboard-accessible navigation, and touch targets of at least 44px.
